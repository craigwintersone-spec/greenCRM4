Searches the live web for currently open funding contracts and grants. A Quality Supervisor agent checks every result before you see it. Then writes your EOI.

Generates authentic social media content using your real impact data — participant numbers, outcomes, quotes, volunteer hours. LinkedIn, X, Instagram, Facebook.

Scans all user-generated text in real time — case notes, referrals, feedback — for language that may breach the Equality Act 2010 or GDPR. Warns advisors and logs to managers.

  • Soft warning before saving
  • Logged to manager review queue
  • GDPR audit trail
  • Pattern + AI detection

Outcomes Analyst Agent On demand
Spots patterns in your caseload — what barriers cluster together, which advisors have the highest outcome rates, where to focus your energy next.
  • Barrier pattern analysis
