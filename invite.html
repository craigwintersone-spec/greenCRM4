Role descriptions
🟢 Admin
Full access to everything. Manages billing, contracts, team members, and all participant data. Typically the programme director or CEO.
🔵 Manager
Can see all participants and generate reports. Cannot manage billing or team members. Ideal for programme managers and senior advisors.
🟣 Advisor
Can only see participants assigned to them. Can log case notes, WhatsApp messages, and update stages. Cannot access reports or contracts.
🟡 Read-only
Can only view funder reports and the impact wall. Cannot see individual participant data. Ideal for funders, trustees, and board members.
