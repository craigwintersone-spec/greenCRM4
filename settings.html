Navigation modules
Turn sections on or off. Hidden sections won't appear in the sidebar for any user in your organisation. Core modules cannot be turned off.
Overview
Dashboard Core
Main overview with stats, at-risk participants, and activity feed.
RAG Status Dashboard
Red/Amber/Green contract performance tracking. Most useful for organisations with multiple funders.
Impact Wall
Animated board-ready stats for funder presentations and trustee meetings.
People
Participants & Beneficiaries Core
Main participant tracking, case notes, and journey management.
Contacts & Donors
Track donors, partner contacts, and supporter relationships.
Volunteers & Staff
Volunteer profiles, skills tracking, and hours logging.
Employer CRM
Track employer relationships, vacancies, and job placements. Useful for employability programmes.
Delivery
Caseload Pipeline
Kanban view of all participants by stage. Essential for advisors managing a caseload.
Referrals
Internal referral tracking with stalled referral alerts.
Partner Referrals
Referrals submitted via the Partner Portal (Probation, Jobcentre etc.).
Events & Workshops
Event creation, capacity tracking, and attendee management.
Circular Economy & Repair
Item tracking, carbon calculator, and repair cohort management. Turn off if not a repair/reuse organisation.
Reporting
Outcomes & Impact Reporting
Outcome tracking, barriers analysis, and good job tracker.
Funding & Contracts
Contract management, targets vs actuals, and underperformance alerts.
Funder Report Generator Pro
One-click MoJ, GLA, CBF, and UKSPF reports.
Evidence Hub
Evidence uploads, audit trail, and missing evidence alerts.
Safeguarding
Flagged cases, consent tracker, and GDPR compliance tools.
