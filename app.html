About equality monitoring
Completion of equality monitoring is voluntary for participants. Data is stored separately from case records, used only for anonymised reporting, and never shared with funders at individual level. GDPR Article 9 compliant.

💚Staff Wellbeing Flag · Weekly scan
Scans advisor case notes for language patterns suggesting stress, burnout, or compassion fatigue. Never shown to the advisor — manager-only. Suggests a quiet check-in when patterns emerge.
How it works
The agent looks for repeated patterns — not single phrases — across an advisor's notes over time. Signs include: language suggesting overwhelm, repeated crisis descriptions, declining note quality, reduced empathy markers. A flag suggests a check-in conversation, not a disciplinary action. This is never shown to the advisor themselves.
🏆Anonymised Benchmarking
Compares your inclusive language score and outcome equity against anonymised aggregates from similar GreenCRM organisations. No org names ever shared.
