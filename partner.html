How the referral process works
1
Submit a referral
Complete the New referral form with the person's details, primary need, and confirm consent. Urgent referrals are flagged for same-day or 48-hour response.
2
Allocation
The programme team reviews and allocates an advisor within the agreed timeframe. Status updates to Engaged once contact is made.
3
Delivery & support
The participant receives tailored support. Track their progress under My referrals. Status updates in real time.
4
Outcome achieved
When an outcome is achieved the status updates. You'll see this reflected in your referral dashboard.
5
Sustainment & closure
The team supports sustainment for 12+ weeks where possible. Cases are closed when support ends.
